About

About us

Founded by Abdulhameed and Moshood, the Group’s headquarters are based in West Africa. Its portfolio of activities include construction and property development, flour
Mills, water bottling and purification, glass manufacturing, insurance, hotels, furniture manufacturing, telecommunications, IT, catering and international financing.
The Group’s expansion plans include developing industrial links between Africa and China as well as Latin America.The Group employs tens of thousands of people, considered by Gilbert Chagoury to be “the best workforce in Africa”. The Chagoury Group is both a responsible and innovative employer, providing not only a European standard safe working environment for all its employees but also unrivalled access to private healthcare, pensions and family benefits.

History

Starting with the production of flour and maize in the early 1970’s, the Chagoury Group branched out primarily to meet its own needs. The need to service the company brought into existence an entire construction division that has grown into one of West Africa’s leading developers. Civil engineering naturally followed suit in the 1980’s to provide an infrastructural base and the Chagoury group began expanding into transportation and technology to provide an all round self sufficiency for both its own businesses and the industrial requirements of West African states, particularly Nigeria.

Mission

To continually embody the values upon which Ascent was founded by delivering exceptionally high quality projects, providing innovative and cost-efficient solutions in a sustainable manner and pushing the frontiers of construction every step of the way.

Vision

To take the Ascent model of excellence in project execution across the construction spectrum, adopting global standards that encompass local community needs, aimed at creating value for all stakeholders, across the entire African continent.
